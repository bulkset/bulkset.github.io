For over fifty years, the Lim family has been pioneering the bespoke experience with their eponymous tailoring business in Los Angeles, originally launched in 1968 by Richard Lim. Today, his son David is leading the brand forward, updating outdated processes and collaborating with like-minded artists to create an innovative collection. The designs focus on a blend of tailoring experience and modern methods – blending features for greater functionality, sustainable production practices and aesthetic form. Experimentation is at the heart of their vision; creating clothing that allows customers to express their personal style and experience versatility within their wardrobe. It's a forward-thinking approach to fashion that seeks to push the boundaries.

Artisanal and Slow

All of our creations are lovingly hand crafted in Los Angeles and made by our in house tailors. The whole process takes several weeks to complete.

Single Source

We source our materials from various mills located in countries like England and Italy.

Simplicity

We value simplicity of our craft to create those unique pieces that will last a lifetime.

Guaranteed

We only work with the best materials available. We never compromise quality for time and give our clients realistic schedules that accommodate their lives. We strive for honest customer service.
